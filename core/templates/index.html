ABOUT US

Greenwald Sales & Marketing is a manufacturers’ representative agency for the foodservice industry’s premier manufacturers in the categories of tabletop, smallwares, and light equipment. Branch office sales and marketing coverage includes Ohio, Indiana, Michigan, Kentucky, West Virginia, and Western Pennsylvania. Melissa Greenwald founded Greenwald Sales & Marketing in 2017 to provide the best representation in the marketplace.

MELISSA GREENWALD,
PRESIDENT

CENTRAL OHIO

Melissa Greenwald is a Manufacturers’ Representative for the foodservice industry’s leading smallwares, tabletop, and light equipment products. Melissa began her career in foodservice equipment and supply sales in 2005. Since then, she has represented more than 50 leading brands, bringing great expertise in the smallwares and tabletop category. In addition, she is the recipient of 4 Performance in Tabletop Awards from Foodservice Equipment & Supplies Magazine. She is a second-generation rep, her father having blazed the same trail for over 30 years before her.

Melissa currently serves on the board of directors for the Manufacturers’ Agents Association for the Foodservice Industry (MAFSI) and had also served on its board from 2011-2016.  In that role, she is the Marketing and Communications Committee Chair and is responsible for the organization’s communications, including the publication of MAFSI Matters.
